Heronet

Amy Lynn Marble is the author of The Adventures of Justice Girl, the start of a young adult superhero series. She graduated from Weber State University with a degree in teaching English and History. She is currently a full-time Engish teacher for a Utah high school. She is excited to present her own spin of her beloved superhero genre.
